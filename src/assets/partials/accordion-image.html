Why a website is important

A website gives your product an online presence. Whether you wish to promote your business, or showcase your work for employment opportunities, having an online presence can help people discover your product, business or even you as an individual. Not only this, but it is an opportunity to show people what the quality of what you offer whether that's the beautiful latte art on your coffee or the way you can revamp some copywriting. You can also share important information such as an address, opening hours or contact deals.
